# Attribution

This pack bundles audio from two upstream sources. Both allow free commercial
use; one (SDAP) requires credit.

## Human voices (folders: alex, ian, sean, karen, meghan)

**Source:** Super Dialogue Audio Pack v1 by Dillon Becker
https://dillonbecker.itch.io/sdap

**License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

**Required credit (paste into your game's credits or about screen):**

> Voice clips by Dillon Becker (Super Dialogue Audio Pack) — CC BY 4.0
> Voice actors: Alex Brodie, Ian Lampert, Sean Lenhart, Karen Cenon, Meghan Christian

The pack has been modified: clips were trimmed of silence, normalised in
loudness, capped at ~600ms with a soft fade-out, and converted from WAV to
OGG. (CC BY 4.0 permits modification; you only need to attribute and indicate
that changes were made.)

## Synthetic syllable banks (folders: pixel_lowest, pixel_low, pixel_med, pixel_high)

**Source:** equalo-official/animalese-generator
https://github.com/equalo-official/animalese-generator

**License:** MIT — no attribution strictly required, but appreciated.

The MIT license text is preserved in `LICENSE-animalese.txt`.

## What is NOT in this pack

The actual voice files from The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom are
copyrighted Nintendo audio. They are *not* included and should not be shipped
in a commercial or public game.
